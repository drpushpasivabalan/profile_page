About Me

I am Dr. R. Pushpa Lakshmi, a dedicated academician and researcher in Computer Science and Engineering. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Network Security. Over the years, I have actively contributed to fostering innovation and mentoring young talent. I have served as SPOC for the IDE Bootcamp, Smart India Hackathon, guided projects under the Naalaya Mudhalvan initiative, acted as an evaluator in Toycathon, and facilitated Google Developer Student Clubs for eight years. I have secured AICTE and CSIR grants to conduct Faculty Development Programs and workshops, and I continue to apply for research funding from various national ministries. I also serve as a reviewer and editorial board member for reputed journals such as Springer and IGI Global, and have chaired sessions at multiple national and international conferences. Through successful collaborations with industry and academia, including MoUs with Zoho Corporation and Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, I strive to nurture student talent in programming, research, and innovation, while contributing to the growth of the Cyber Security domain.
